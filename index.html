30-Day Basketball Workout Plan

This progressive plan builds your skills step-by-step over 4 weeks. Each week gets more intense!

Week 1 – Fundamentals

  • Day 1: Warm-up, Stationary Dribbling, Form Shooting, Jump Rope (2x1 min)
  • Day 2: Cone Dribbles, Spot Shooting (5 spots x 5), Lateral Slides
  • Day 3: Figure 8 Dribbling, Catch & Shoot, Sprint Intervals (4x20m)
  • Day 4: Ball Slaps & Pound Dribbles, Free Throws (50), Defensive Closeouts
  • Day 5: 2-Ball Dribble (Stationary), Form Shooting, Jump Rope (3x1 min)
  • Day 6: Review Drills + Mini Scrimmage Simulation
  • Day 7: Rest & Recovery

Week 2 – Skill Reinforcement

  • Day 1: Cone Weave Dribbles, Mid-range Shooting, Suicide Runs (3x)
  • Day 2: Behind-the-Back Dribbles, Free Throws (75), Footwork Ladder (if available)
  • Day 3: 1-2 Pull-up Shooting, Crossover + Drive, Plank Hold (3x30s)
  • Day 4: Dribble + Reaction, 3-Spot Shooting, Wall Sit (2x1 min)
  • Day 5: Hesitation Dribbles, Catch-Shoot-Pivot, Cone Slides
  • Day 6: Scrimmage Practice or 1v1 Situations
  • Day 7: Active Rest (light jog/stretch)

Week 3 – Game Intensity

  • Day 1: Zig-Zag Dribbling, Pull-Up Jumper, Full-Court Sprints (6x)
  • Day 2: Euro Step Drills, Catch & Shoot (10 reps per spot), Jump Rope (4x1 min)
  • Day 3: Spin Move Dribbles, 3-point Shooting (5x5), Closeouts + Box-out
  • Day 4: Crossover Series, Midrange Fakes, Agility Ladder
  • Day 5: Game-Speed Shooting, Hesitation & Attack, Wall Sit (3x1 min)
  • Day 6: Simulated Game Drills or 1v1 Play
  • Day 7: Stretch, Foam Roll, Light Shooting

Week 4 – Performance Challenge

  • Day 1: Combo Dribbles (3-move), Shooting Under Fatigue, Jump Rope (5x1 min)
  • Day 2: Speed Layup Drill, Catch & Shoot (Speed Round), Sprint/Backpedal x5
  • Day 3: Full Dribble Series, 3-point Challenge, Core Circuit (Sit-ups, Planks)
  • Day 4: Pressure Free Throws (simulate game), Transition Shooting, Agility Ladder
  • Day 5: Full Court Handles, Defensive Shuffle Races, Finishing Moves
  • Day 6: Mock Game or Video Analysis of Your Progress
  • Day 7: Recovery + Goal Setting
